Different animations while closing Bootstrap 4 alerts

Press on close button on every alert and see the animation.
Default Close! Hide alert by default close action.
Fade Show Close! Fade alert for 2 seconds and close.
Slide Up Close! Slide up for 2 seconds and close.
Animate Left Up! Animate left up for 1 second and close.
Animate Right! Animate right for 1 second and close.
Animate Left! Animate left for 1 second and close.
Animate Text Disappear! Animate text disappear for 1 second and close.
Animate Margin Left! Animate margin left for 1 second and close.
